Cost of Website Design in Nigeria: Pricing Guide 2026

The cost of building a website in Nigeria starts from ₦250,000 and can go up to ₦5,000,000+. This range is quite large because every website differs, and pricing is often based on the scope of the website design project.

1. Informational Website

Perfect for SMEs. Its main purpose is to display information about a business. Usually has 1-5 pages without advanced functionalities.

Examples:

  • • One-page informational: eds-nigeria.com
  • • Five-page informational: pugantechnologies.com

Cost: One-page starts at ₦250,000.

2. E-commerce Website

Digital store allowing users to buy and sell goods online with secure payment gateways like Paystack and Flutterwave.

Cost: Starts at ₦800,000 – ₦1,200,000+.

Key Factors Influencing Costs

1. Type of Website

Informational is affordable, Custom is the most expensive.

2. Features

Payment Integration and Dashboards increase price.

Cost-Saving Tips

  • • Bundle hosting and maintenance.
  • • Start small and scale up as you grow.
