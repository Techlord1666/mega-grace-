Cost of Website Design in Nigeria: Pricing Guide 2026

The cost of building a website in Nigeria starts from ₦250,000 and can go up to ₦5,000,000+. This range is quite large because every website differs, and pricing is often based on the scope of the website design project.

Knowing what to budget for a website can be tricky because price ranges online are often too wide. In this guide, we will analyze:

What Drives the Price?

Type & Complexity

Informational is cheapest; eCommerce requires security and management; Custom requires pure code and logic.

Features & Function

Dashboards, AI Chatbots, and Payment Integrations take more time and expertise to implement, raising the cost.

Design Complexity

Templates are faster and cheaper. Custom designs involve unique layouts tailored specifically to your brand audience.

Domain & Hosting

Domains (₦20k - ₦25k/yr) and Hosting (₦60k - ₦100k/yr) are recurring costs required for your site to live online.

Agency Expertise

Freelancers are cheaper but risky. Professional Agencies like Mega Grace offer long-term support and peace of mind.

Content & SEO

Content Writing in Nigeria costs ₦150k - ₦500k. Professional content guides users to make a buying decision.
