Pricing Guide 2026

Website design in Nigeria starts from ₦250,000 and can go up to ₦5,000,000+. Here is the breakdown:

1. Informational Website

Perfect for SMEs. Main purpose is to display business details. 1-5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact). No payment integration.

Starts: ₦100,000 to ₦750,000 (2 page)–(5 pages).

2. E-commerce Website

Full digital store. Processes orders, accepts payments (Paystack/Flutterwave), and manages automated shipping/inventory.

Starts: ₦200,000 – ₦1,200,000+.

3. Custom Website

Manual code (React, Node, etc.). Unique functionality like school portals or complex marketplaces.

Starts: ₦350,000 to ₦5,000,000+.
